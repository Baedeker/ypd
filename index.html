About

This YP Database is to help the serving ones better able to manage the service and stay up to date with their YP.

How it works:

  • Register and create an account for your locality.
  • When you log into your locality, you'll be able to add YP, along with any details.
  • Additionally, you will also be able to assess the YP's status, and submit any updates for them.
